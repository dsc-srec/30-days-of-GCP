What is 30 Days of Google Cloud ?

30 Days of Google Cloud program will provide you an opportunity to kickstart your career in cloud and get hands-on practice on Google Cloud Platform - the tool that powers apps like Google Search, Gmail and YouTube.

Along the way, you will learn & practice concepts like computing, application development, big data & machine learning using cloud & if you get stuck, you will have your "Campus Facilitators" who are specially trained on Google Cloud to help. üòé

What is DSC SREC ?

Developer Student Clubs are university-based community groups for students interested in technologies. Students from all undergraduate or graduate programs with an interest in growing as a developer are welcome. By joining a DSC, students grow their knowledge in a peer-to-peer learning environment and build solutions for local businesses and their community.

" We LEARN, SHARE, INNOVATE
as a
COMMUNITY"

We here at DSC SREC work on the above principal. Students at DSC SREC strongly believes that when other "grow" we "grow". Building a community that can spread positivity, awareness and love back to the society is the goal of DSC SREC.
